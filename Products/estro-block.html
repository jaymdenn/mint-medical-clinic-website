Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Estro Block?

Estro Block is a physician-formulated supplement designed to support healthy estrogen metabolism and hormonal balance in men. It contains natural compounds like DIM, Calcium D-Glucarate, and Chrysin Extract that help maintain optimal testosterone-to-estrogen ratios.

Why do men need estrogen control?

As testosterone increases (naturally or through TRT), some converts to estrogen through a process called aromatization. Estro Block helps maintain optimal balance by supporting healthy estrogen metabolism pathways, preventing estrogen-related side effects like water retention and mood changes.

How does Estro Block work?

Estro Block contains DIM and other natural compounds that support healthy estrogen metabolism pathways. These ingredients help your body process and eliminate excess estrogen while maintaining the ideal hormonal balance necessary for optimal male health and performance.

Who should take Estro Block?

Estro Block is ideal for men on testosterone replacement therapy (TRT), those with naturally high estrogen levels, or anyone seeking hormonal optimization. It's particularly beneficial for men experiencing symptoms of estrogen dominance like water retention or difficulty losing body fat.

What are signs of high estrogen in men?

Common signs include water retention and bloating, mood changes or irritability, fatigue and low energy, reduced libido, difficulty losing body fat (especially around the midsection), and gynecomastia (breast tissue development). If you're experiencing these symptoms, consult with our physicians.

How long until I see results?

Effects on hormonal balance are typically noticed within 4-6 weeks with consistent use. Many patients report reduced water retention and improved energy within the first few weeks. Optimal results are achieved when combined with proper hormone monitoring by your physician.

Do I need a prescription?

No, Estro Block is a supplement and does not require a prescription. Simply add to cart and order directly. For personalized guidance on dosing or to discuss your hormone optimization goals, our medical team is available for optional consultations.
