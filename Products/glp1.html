Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GLP-1 medication?

GLP-1 medications are glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists (such as semaglutide and tirzepatide) that help with weight loss by reducing appetite and regulating blood sugar. These prescription medications mimic a natural hormone your body produces to control hunger and metabolism.

How does GLP-1 help with weight loss?

GLP-1 mimics the natural GLP-1 hormone in your body, which reduces hunger signals, slows gastric emptying (how quickly food leaves your stomach), and helps you feel full longer. This combination makes it easier to eat less and maintain a calorie deficit for sustainable weight loss.

How much weight can I lose?

Clinical studies show that patients typically lose 10-20% of their body weight over several months with GLP-1 therapy. Individual results vary based on factors like starting weight, adherence to the protocol, diet, and exercise habits. Our medical team monitors your progress and adjusts your treatment for optimal results.

How is GLP-1 administered?

GLP-1 is administered as a once-weekly self-injection into fatty tissue (typically the belly, thigh, or upper arm). The injections use a small, thin needle similar to insulin pens. Our team trains you on proper injection technique during your consultation, and most patients find the process quick and comfortable.

What are the side effects?

The most common side effects include nausea, which typically improves over time as your body adjusts to the medication. Other potential side effects may include constipation, diarrhea, or decreased appetite. Our physician monitors your progress closely and can adjust your dosing schedule to minimize side effects while maximizing results.

Who qualifies for GLP-1?

Generally, candidates have a BMI of 30 or higher, or a BMI of 27 or higher with weight-related health conditions such as high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, or high cholesterol. During your telehealth consultation, our physician will review your medical history and determine if GLP-1 therapy is appropriate for your specific situation.

Do I need ongoing monitoring?

Yes, regular check-ins with your physician are essential for safe and effective treatment. Our medical team monitors your progress, adjusts dosing as needed, manages any side effects, and ensures you're achieving optimal results. This ongoing medical supervision is a key component of our GLP-1 weight loss program.
