Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vaso Pump?

Vaso Pump is a physician-formulated vasodilator supplement that promotes healthy blood flow and circulation through nitric oxide support. It contains natural ingredients like L-Citrulline, L-Arginine, and Beetroot Extract designed to enhance cardiovascular health and physical performance.

How does Vaso Pump work?

Vaso Pump contains natural ingredients that boost nitric oxide production in your body. Nitric oxide helps relax and widen blood vessels (vasodilation), which improves blood flow and circulation throughout the body, including to muscles and vital organs.

What are the benefits of Vaso Pump?

Key benefits include enhanced blood flow and circulation, improved physical performance and workout results, cardiovascular system support, better oxygen and nutrient delivery to muscles and tissues, and improved intimacy health through better blood flow.

Who should take Vaso Pump?

Vaso Pump is ideal for men seeking improved circulation, physical performance support, cardiovascular wellness, or enhanced blood flow for intimacy health. It's particularly beneficial for active individuals, those with circulation concerns, or men looking to support their overall cardiovascular function.

How long until I see results from Vaso Pump?

Many users notice improved energy and circulation within 2-4 weeks of consistent use. For optimal results, take Vaso Pump daily as directed by your physician. Individual results may vary based on your overall health and lifestyle factors.

Can I take Vaso Pump with ED medications?

You should consult your physician before combining Vaso Pump with ED medications, as both affect blood flow and blood pressure. Your doctor will evaluate your complete health profile and current medications to determine if the combination is safe and appropriate for you.

Do I need a consultation for Vaso Pump?

Yes, Vaso Pump is available through telehealth consultation with our physicians. This ensures the supplement is safe for you and won't interact with your current medications or health conditions. Schedule a virtual consultation to get started.
