What is Test Boost?

Test Boost is a physician-formulated testosterone support supplement containing natural ingredients like Tribulus, Fenugreek, D-Aspartic Acid, Zinc, Vitamin D3, and Ashwagandha designed to optimize natural hormone production. It helps men support healthy testosterone levels, increase energy, enhance muscle recovery, and improve overall vitality.

How does Test Boost work?

Test Boost contains scientifically-backed ingredients including Tribulus Terrestris, Fenugreek, D-Aspartic Acid, Zinc, Vitamin D3, and Ashwagandha that work together to support your body's natural testosterone production. These ingredients help optimize hormone levels, enhance energy, and support muscle recovery without synthetic hormones.

Who should take Test Boost?

Test Boost is designed for men over 30 experiencing low energy, reduced libido, difficulty building muscle, or seeking natural hormone optimization. It's ideal for active individuals wanting better recovery and men looking to optimize their vitality through natural supplementation under physician supervision.

How long until I see results from Test Boost?

Most users notice improvements in energy levels and mood within 4-6 weeks of consistent daily use. Optimal results for muscle recovery, strength, and overall vitality typically appear after 8-12 weeks as your body's natural testosterone production optimizes.

Are there side effects from Test Boost?

Test Boost is generally well-tolerated when used as directed. However, you should consult with a physician before starting, especially if you have pre-existing medical conditions or take other medications. Your Mint Medical physician will evaluate potential interactions and ensure it's safe for you.

Do I need a consultation for Test Boost?

Yes, Test Boost is available through a telehealth consultation with our physicians. This ensures the supplement is appropriate for your health status and allows for proper monitoring of your hormone optimization journey.

Can I take Test Boost with other supplements?

Your physician will advise on proper supplement stacking during your consultation. Test Boost pairs well with Vaso Pump for enhanced blood flow and Estro Block for healthy estrogen balance. Always consult your doctor before combining supplements to avoid interactions.
