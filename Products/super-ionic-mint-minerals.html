Frequently Asked Questions

What are Super Ionic Mint Minerals?

Super Ionic Mint Minerals is a comprehensive ionic mineral supplement providing essential trace minerals in highly bioavailable form. The ionic formulation allows for rapid cellular absorption, ensuring your body gets the minerals it needs for optimal function.

Why are ionic minerals important?

Ionic minerals are more easily absorbed by the body compared to standard mineral supplements. Because they're already in an electrically charged state, they can pass through cell membranes more efficiently, delivering faster and more complete absorption.

What minerals are included?

Our formula contains a full spectrum of essential trace minerals including ionic magnesium, potassium citrate, calcium, sodium bicarbonate, zinc, selenium, and a complete trace mineral complex for comprehensive cellular support.

What are the benefits?

Super Ionic Mint Minerals supports energy production, immune function, muscle function, and overall cellular health. It enhances hydration, improves recovery, supports nerve and brain function, and helps maintain optimal electrolyte balance.

Who should take ionic minerals?

Anyone seeking to optimize mineral intake, those with dietary deficiencies, active individuals, athletes, people on weight loss programs (especially GLP-1 patients), or anyone experiencing fatigue or dehydration can benefit from ionic mineral supplementation.

How do I take them?

Add 1-2 droppers to 8-16 oz of water and take 1-3 times daily, or as needed for hydration support. Follow physician dosing instructions if provided. Increase intake during exercise, hot weather, or when on GLP-1 medications.
