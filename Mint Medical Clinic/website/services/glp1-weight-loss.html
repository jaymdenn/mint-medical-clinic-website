What are GLP-1 Medications?

GLP-1 receptor agonists like semaglutide (Ozempic®, Wegovy®) and tirzepatide (Mounjaro®, Zepbound®) are FDA-approved medications that help regulate appetite and blood sugar. Originally developed for diabetes, they've proven remarkably effective for weight loss.

These medications work by mimicking a hormone that signals fullness to your brain, reducing hunger and helping you feel satisfied with smaller portions.

  • Average 15-20% body weight loss
  • Reduced appetite and cravings
  • Improved blood sugar control
  • Once-weekly injection
  • Physician-supervised program
