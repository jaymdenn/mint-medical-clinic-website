What is Acoustic Wave Therapy?

Acoustic Wave Therapy (AWT) uses low-intensity sound waves to stimulate blood vessel growth and improve blood flow to the penis. Unlike medications that temporarily mask symptoms, AWT addresses the root cause of ED by promoting natural healing and regeneration.

The treatment works by triggering a process called neovascularization — the formation of new blood vessels. This improves circulation, which is essential for achieving and maintaining erections naturally.
