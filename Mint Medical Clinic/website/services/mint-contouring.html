What is Mint Contouring?

Mint Contouring uses advanced non-invasive technology to target and reduce stubborn fat in problem areas. The treatment works by disrupting fat cells, which are then naturally eliminated by your body over the following weeks.

Perfect for those trouble spots that don't respond to diet and exercise — like love handles, belly fat, thighs, and arms.
