What are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules in your body. Think of them as messengers that communicate with your cells, triggering specific biological responses like tissue repair, hormone production, muscle growth, and cellular regeneration.

Unlike proteins, which are long chains of amino acids, peptides are smaller and more targeted in their function. This makes them highly effective at delivering precise signals to optimize various bodily processes.

All peptide therapies at Mint Medical Clinic are physician-supervised and carefully monitored. While peptides show promising benefits, they are used under medical supervision and are not FDA-approved for specific conditions.
