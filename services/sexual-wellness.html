Frequently Asked Questions About Sexual Wellness

What is sexual wellness?

Sexual wellness encompasses physical, emotional, and relational aspects of sexual health. It includes treating conditions like ED and low libido, optimizing hormones, and enhancing intimacy and satisfaction for individuals and couples.

What sexual wellness services do you offer for men?

For men, we offer acoustic wave therapy for ED, P-Shot PRP therapy, testosterone replacement therapy, ED medications, premature ejaculation treatment, and Peyronie's disease treatment.

What sexual wellness services do you offer for women?

For women, we offer O-Shot PRP therapy, hormone replacement therapy, treatments for low libido and HSDD, menopause symptom management, and vaginal rejuvenation therapies.

Is sexual wellness treatment confidential?

Absolutely. We maintain strict HIPAA compliance and all consultations and treatments are completely confidential. Our private consultation rooms ensure discretion and comfort.

Can couples receive treatment together?

Yes, we encourage couples to address intimacy concerns together. We offer couples consultations and can create complementary treatment plans for both partners to optimize your intimate relationship.

How do I know if I need sexual wellness treatment?

If you're experiencing decreased libido, difficulty with arousal or performance, pain during intercourse, reduced satisfaction, or hormone imbalance symptoms, you may benefit from treatment. We offer free consultations to discuss your concerns.

Are sexual wellness treatments covered by insurance?

Coverage varies by treatment and insurance plan. Some hormone therapies may be covered. We offer competitive self-pay pricing and financing options through LiteCredit to make treatment accessible.
