What is the P-Shot?

The P-Shot (Priapus Shot) is a cutting-edge regenerative medicine treatment that uses platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from your own blood to rejuvenate penile tissue and improve sexual function.

Named after Priapus, the Greek god of fertility, this procedure harnesses your body's natural healing capabilities. Platelets contain growth factors that stimulate tissue regeneration, new blood vessel formation, and nerve repair—addressing ED at its source.

Benefits of the P-Shot

  • Stronger, firmer erections
  • Enhanced sensitivity and pleasure
  • Improved sexual stamina
  • Natural treatment using your own blood
  • No drugs or synthetic materials
  • Quick procedure with minimal downtime
  • Can be combined with other treatments
The Process

How the P-Shot Works

1

Blood Draw

A small amount of blood is drawn from your arm, similar to a routine blood test.

2

PRP Processing

Your blood is placed in a centrifuge to separate and concentrate the platelet-rich plasma containing growth factors.

3

Numbing

A topical anesthetic cream and/or local nerve block is applied to ensure complete comfort during the procedure.

4

PRP Injection

The PRP is carefully injected into specific areas of the penis where it triggers tissue regeneration and new blood vessel growth.

The Science

How PRP Stimulates Regeneration

Growth Factors

Platelets release powerful growth factors including PDGF, VEGF, and TGF-β that signal your body to generate new, healthy tissue and blood vessels.

Neovascularization

New blood vessel formation improves blood flow to the penis—the same mechanism that makes acoustic wave therapy effective. Better blood flow means better erections.

Nerve Regeneration

Growth factors stimulate nerve repair and regeneration, leading to enhanced sensitivity and improved sensation during intimacy.

Tissue Repair

PRP promotes the repair of damaged penile tissue, which can help reverse some of the effects of aging, diabetes, and other conditions that affect function.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About the P-Shot

What is the P-Shot (Priapus Shot)?

The P-Shot is a regenerative medicine treatment that uses platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from your own blood to stimulate tissue regeneration in the penis. The growth factors in PRP promote new blood vessel formation, nerve regeneration, and tissue repair to improve erectile function and sensation.

How does the P-Shot work?

Blood is drawn from your arm and processed in a centrifuge to concentrate the platelets. This platelet-rich plasma contains growth factors that, when injected into specific areas of the penis, trigger tissue regeneration, improve blood flow, and enhance sensitivity.

Is the P-Shot painful?

Most patients experience minimal discomfort. A topical numbing cream is applied before the procedure, and many patients also receive a local nerve block for complete comfort. The injection itself takes only a few minutes.

What results can I expect from the P-Shot?

Benefits include stronger, firmer erections, improved sensitivity and sensation, enhanced sexual stamina, and potentially increased size due to improved blood flow. Some patients notice effects within weeks, with full results developing over 2-3 months.

How long do P-Shot results last?

Results typically last 12-18 months. Many patients choose to have maintenance treatments annually to sustain benefits. Results can be enhanced when combined with acoustic wave therapy or other treatments.

Is the P-Shot safe?

Yes, the P-Shot is very safe because it uses your own blood plasma with no synthetic drugs or foreign substances. There's no risk of allergic reaction or rejection. Minor bruising or swelling may occur but resolves quickly.

Who is a good candidate for the P-Shot?

The P-Shot is ideal for men experiencing erectile dysfunction, reduced sensation, or those wanting enhanced sexual performance. It works well as a standalone treatment or in combination with acoustic wave therapy or medications for comprehensive results.
