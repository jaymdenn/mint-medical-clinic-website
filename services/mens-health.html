Frequently Asked Questions

What services does a men's health clinic offer?

Men's health clinics like Mint Medical specialize in erectile dysfunction treatment, testosterone replacement therapy, sexual wellness, hormone optimization, weight management, and addressing conditions that affect men's vitality and quality of life.

Why should I go to a men's health clinic instead of my regular doctor?

Men's health clinics specialize exclusively in conditions affecting men's vitality and sexual health. Our focused expertise means more treatment options, deeper understanding of these conditions, and a comfortable environment designed specifically for men's needs.

Is treatment at a men's clinic confidential?

Yes, all treatment is 100% confidential and HIPAA compliant. We understand the sensitive nature of men's health issues and maintain strict privacy protocols. Our clinics are designed for discretion.

What should I expect at my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive consultation, review of symptoms and health history, diagnostic testing (including blood work and ultrasound if needed), and a personalized treatment plan. Most consultations take 30-60 minutes.

Do I need a referral to visit Mint Medical Clinic?

No referral is needed. You can schedule directly with us for a free, confidential consultation. We accept patients from all over Utah and welcome self-referrals.

Does insurance cover men's health treatments?

Coverage varies by insurance plan and treatment type. Some treatments like testosterone therapy may be covered. We offer affordable self-pay options and financing through LiteCredit to ensure treatment is accessible.

What age groups do you treat?

We treat adult men of all ages, from their 20s to their 80s. ED and hormone issues can affect men at any age, and effective treatments are available regardless of age.
