What is Acoustic Wave Therapy?

Acoustic Wave Therapy (AWT) uses low-intensity sound waves to stimulate blood vessel growth and improve blood flow to the penis. Unlike medications that temporarily mask symptoms, AWT addresses the root cause of ED by promoting natural healing and regeneration.

The treatment works by triggering a process called neovascularization — the formation of new blood vessels. This improves circulation, which is essential for achieving and maintaining erections naturally.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Acoustic Wave Therapy for ED?

Acoustic Wave Therapy (AWT) uses low-intensity sound waves to stimulate blood vessel growth (neovascularization) and improve blood flow. Unlike medications that temporarily mask symptoms, AWT addresses the root cause of erectile dysfunction by promoting natural healing and regeneration.

How long does a treatment session take?

Each session takes 20-25 minutes with treatments delivered in blocks of 2000 pulses. A typical treatment protocol involves 6-12 sessions scheduled over 2-3 weeks for optimal results.

Is Acoustic Wave Therapy painful?

No, it's non-invasive and generally comfortable with zero downtime. Patients can resume normal activities immediately after each session without any recovery period needed.

How soon will I see results?

Most patients notice improvements within 2-3 weeks of starting treatment, with optimal results appearing 4-12 weeks after completing the full protocol as neovascularization occurs.

How long do results last?

Results can last 2+ years. The treatment promotes the formation of new blood vessels, providing long-lasting improvement. Some patients may benefit from annual maintenance treatments to sustain optimal results.

Who is a good candidate for Acoustic Wave Therapy?

AWT is best for men with mild to moderate ED. Men with vascular issues from diabetes, high blood pressure, or obesity can particularly benefit. The treatment works for patients of any age seeking a non-invasive solution.

Is Acoustic Wave Therapy FDA approved for ED?

It's FDA approved for sports medicine and orthopedic applications. It is used off-label for erectile dysfunction treatment based on strong clinical data and research showing significant benefits for ED patients.
