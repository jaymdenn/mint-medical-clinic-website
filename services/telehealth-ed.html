Treatment Options

FDA-Approved Medications We Prescribe

All medications are PDE5 inhibitors that work by increasing blood flow to help you achieve and maintain erections.

Sildenafil (Generic Viagra)

Fast-acting medication taken 30-60 minutes before intimacy. Effects last 4-6 hours. Available in multiple dosages tailored to your needs.

Tadalafil (Generic Cialis)

Long-lasting option with effects up to 36 hours. Can be taken daily at low doses for spontaneity, or as-needed at higher doses.

Custom Compounds

Personalized formulations including troches (sublingual tablets) and topical creams for patients who prefer alternatives to oral pills.

Combination Therapy

Multi-medication approaches combining PDE5 inhibitors with supplements or other therapies for enhanced results when single medications aren't sufficient.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does telehealth ED treatment work?

Schedule a confidential video visit with a licensed physician who reviews your medical history and discusses your symptoms. If appropriate, the physician prescribes FDA-approved ED medication tailored to your individual needs. The process is 100% private and conducted via secure, HIPAA-compliant platform.

What medications can be prescribed?

FDA-approved ED medications including sildenafil (generic Viagra) and tadalafil (generic Cialis) can be prescribed based on your individual needs and medical history. These are PDE5 inhibitors that help improve blood flow for achieving and maintaining erections.

How quickly can I get my medication?

After your telehealth visit is complete and a prescription is approved, medications ship directly to your door in discreet, plain packaging. Most orders arrive within 48 hours, though delivery times may vary based on your location.

Is telehealth ED treatment safe?

Yes, you're consulting with licensed U.S. physicians who thoroughly review your medical history, current medications, and health conditions to ensure ED medication is safe and appropriate for you. The platform is HIPAA-compliant and all consultations are completely confidential.

What if I need a physical exam?

While many ED cases can be evaluated virtually, we may recommend an in-person visit if you have certain cardiovascular risk factors, don't achieve erections at all, or if your medical history suggests the need for additional testing. Our clinics in Sandy and Layton offer comprehensive in-person evaluations including blood flow ultrasound.

How much does telehealth ED cost?

Telehealth ED treatment offers competitive pricing for both virtual consultations and medications, making it much more affordable than traditional in-person specialty visits. Exact pricing depends on medication type and dosage selected. Contact us at (801) 804-8000 for current pricing details.

Is my information kept private?

Absolutely. All virtual visits use HIPAA-compliant encrypted platforms that protect your medical information. Medications ship in plain, discreet packaging with no indication of contents. Your privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities.
