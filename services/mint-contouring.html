What is Mint Contouring?

Mint Contouring uses advanced non-invasive fat reduction technologies to target and eliminate stubborn fat in problem areas. Our state-of-the-art treatments employ cryolipolysis (controlled cooling), radiofrequency (heat), and laser technology to destroy fat cells without surgery, needles, or incisions.

The treatment works by disrupting fat cells in targeted areas, which your body then naturally processes and eliminates through your lymphatic system over the following weeks. The destroyed fat cells are gone permanently and do not regenerate.

Perfect for those trouble spots that don't respond to diet and exercise — including abdomen, love handles, thighs, chin, upper arms, and back fat. Ideal for patients near their goal weight who want to sculpt and refine their body shape.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is non-surgical body contouring?

Non-surgical body contouring uses technology-based treatments that destroy fat cells without surgery. These treatments use cold (cryolipolysis), heat (radiofrequency), or ultrasound to target stubborn fat deposits that your body then naturally processes and eliminates over the following weeks.

How does body contouring work?

Body contouring treatments work by damaging or destroying fat cells in targeted areas. Once these fat cells are disrupted, your body's natural processes break them down and eliminate them through your lymphatic system over several weeks. The destroyed fat cells are gone permanently and do not regenerate.

What areas can be treated with body contouring?

Common treatment areas include the abdomen, love handles (flanks), thighs (inner and outer), chin (submental fat), upper arms, and back fat. Any area with stubborn fat deposits that resist diet and exercise can potentially be treated.

How much fat can I lose with body contouring?

Results vary by individual, but most patients experience 20-25% fat reduction per treated area. You can expect approximately 2-4 cm (about 1-2 inches) of circumference reduction in the treated area. This is not a weight loss treatment but rather a body sculpting solution for specific problem areas.

How many treatments will I need?

Most patients achieve their desired results with 1-3 treatments per area, though this varies based on your goals and the amount of fat in the target area. Treatments are typically spaced several weeks apart to allow your body time to naturally eliminate the destroyed fat cells.

Is there downtime after body contouring?

One of the major benefits of non-surgical body contouring is little to no downtime. Most patients return to their normal activities immediately after treatment. Some may experience temporary redness, mild swelling, or tenderness in the treated area, but these effects typically resolve within a few days.

Who is a good candidate for body contouring?

The ideal candidate is someone who is near their goal weight (typically BMI under 30) with stubborn fat pockets that resist diet and exercise. Body contouring is not a weight loss solution but rather a way to sculpt and refine your body shape. You should be in overall good health and have realistic expectations about the results.
