What is TRT?

Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT) is medical treatment that supplements your body's natural testosterone production when levels fall below optimal. By restoring testosterone to healthy levels, TRT reverses the symptoms of low T and helps you feel like yourself again.

At Mint Medical Clinic, we take a comprehensive approach to TRT—starting with thorough testing to understand your complete hormonal picture, then creating a personalized protocol designed for your specific needs and goals.

Benefits of TRT

  • Increased energy and reduced fatigue
  • Improved libido and sexual function
  • Increased muscle mass and strength
  • Reduced body fat, especially belly fat
  • Better mood and reduced depression
  • Improved mental clarity and focus
  • Better sleep quality
  • Stronger bones and reduced osteoporosis risk
Treatment Options

TRT Delivery Methods

We offer multiple ways to administer testosterone based on your preferences and lifestyle.

Testosterone Injections

The most common and cost-effective method. Weekly or bi-weekly intramuscular injections of testosterone cypionate provide stable levels and reliable results.

  • Most effective delivery method
  • Flexible dosing
  • Self-administered at home

Topical Testosterone

Daily application of testosterone gel or cream to the skin. Good option for those who prefer not to inject.

  • No injections required
  • Daily application
  • Steady hormone levels

Testosterone Pellets

Tiny pellets implanted under the skin that release testosterone over 4-6 months. Maximum convenience with minimal maintenance.

  • Longest-lasting option
  • In-office procedure
  • Very consistent levels

HCG Therapy

Often combined with TRT to maintain testicular function, natural production, and fertility. Important for men who want to preserve fertility.

  • Preserves fertility
  • Maintains testicular size
  • Supports natural production

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About TRT

What is TRT (Testosterone Replacement Therapy)?

TRT is medical treatment that supplements your body's natural testosterone production when levels are low. It's administered through injections, gels, creams, or pellets to restore testosterone to optimal levels and relieve symptoms of low T.

What are the benefits of TRT?

Benefits include increased energy and reduced fatigue, improved libido and sexual function, increased muscle mass and strength, reduced body fat, better mood and mental clarity, improved sleep quality, and stronger bones.

How is TRT administered?

TRT can be administered via weekly or bi-weekly intramuscular injections (most common), daily topical gels or creams, testosterone pellets implanted under the skin every 4-6 months, or subcutaneous injections. We help you choose the best method for your lifestyle.

How quickly will I see results from TRT?

Many patients notice improved energy and mood within 2-3 weeks. Libido improvements typically occur within 3-6 weeks. Changes in body composition (muscle gain, fat loss) become noticeable after 3-6 months of consistent treatment.

Is TRT safe?

TRT is safe when properly monitored by experienced medical professionals. We perform regular blood work to monitor hormone levels, hematocrit, PSA, and other health markers, adjusting your protocol as needed to ensure safety and optimal results.

Will TRT affect my fertility?

TRT can suppress natural testosterone production and reduce sperm production. If fertility is a concern, we can add HCG therapy to maintain testicular function and sperm production while on TRT. We discuss fertility options during your consultation.

How much does TRT cost?
