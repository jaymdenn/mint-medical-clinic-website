The O-Shot: Revolutionary PRP Therapy for Women

The O-Shot (Orgasm Shot) is a non-surgical treatment that uses platelet-rich plasma (PRP) from your own blood to rejuvenate vaginal tissue. This innovative procedure stimulates collagen production, increases blood flow, and regenerates nerve endings to enhance sexual function and sensation.

By injecting concentrated growth factors into specific areas, the O-Shot addresses the root causes of decreased sensitivity, lubrication issues, and diminished sexual response — not just the symptoms. It's ideal for women experiencing perimenopausal or menopausal changes, post-childbirth intimacy issues, or simply seeking enhanced sexual wellness.

  • Heightened sensitivity and sensation
  • Improved natural lubrication
  • Stronger, more intense orgasms
  • Better bladder control
  • Relief from painful intimacy
The Process

How the O-Shot Works

1

Blood Draw

A small amount of blood is drawn from your arm, similar to a routine blood test.

2

PRP Separation

Your blood is processed in a centrifuge to separate the platelet-rich plasma containing concentrated growth factors.

3

Numbing & Injection

A numbing cream is applied for comfort. The PRP is then carefully injected into specific areas to stimulate healing and regeneration.

4

Tissue Regeneration

Over the following weeks and months, new collagen forms, blood flow increases, and nerve endings regenerate for enhanced sensation.

Common Questions

O-Shot & Women's Health FAQ

What is the O-Shot?

The O-Shot is a non-surgical PRP (platelet-rich plasma) treatment that uses your own blood to rejuvenate vaginal tissue. It stimulates collagen production, increases blood flow, and regenerates nerve endings to improve sexual function and sensation.

How does the O-Shot work?

Blood is drawn from your arm, then platelet-rich plasma is separated using a centrifuge. The concentrated PRP is then carefully injected into specific areas to stimulate healing, collagen production, and tissue regeneration.

What benefits can I expect from the O-Shot?

Women typically experience heightened sensitivity, improved natural lubrication, stronger orgasms, better bladder control, and relief from painful intimacy. Results vary by individual but most women report significant improvements in sexual wellness and quality of life.

Is the O-Shot procedure painful?

A numbing cream is applied before the procedure, so most women report minimal discomfort. The entire procedure takes approximately 30 minutes with no downtime required. You can resume normal activities immediately.

How soon will I see results from the O-Shot?

Some women notice immediate improvement in sensitivity and sensation. Full results typically develop over 3 months as new tissue and collagen develop. Benefits continue to improve during this period as blood flow increases and nerve endings regenerate.

How long do O-Shot results last?

Results typically last 14-18 months. Most women choose to repeat the treatment every 18 months for maintenance and continued benefits. The longevity of results can vary based on individual factors and lifestyle.

Is the O-Shot safe?

Yes, the O-Shot is very safe because it uses your own blood, so the risk of rejection or allergic reaction is extremely low. The procedure has been performed safely on thousands of women worldwide. Our medical team follows strict protocols to ensure your safety and comfort.
