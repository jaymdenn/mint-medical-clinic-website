What is Stem Cell Therapy & Why is Stem Cell Therapy for You?

Stem cell therapy, also known as regenerative medicine, promotes the repair response of diseased, dysfunctional, or injured tissue using stem cells or their derivatives. According to Mayo Clinic, stem cells have the potential to be grown to become new tissue for use in transplant and regenerative medicine.

Stem cells are unique because they can develop into many different cell types in the body. When introduced to damaged areas, they can help regenerate and repair tissue by developing into the specific cells needed for healing. Research from Johns Hopkins University shows that stem cells can be encouraged to grow into replacement nerves, soft tissues, muscle, blood vessels, and bone.

Stem cell therapy is for you if you're looking for a treatment that addresses the root cause of your condition—not just the symptoms. Whether you're experiencing intimacy issues, tissue damage, or age-related decline, stem cell therapy offers a natural, regenerative approach to restore function and vitality.

Frequently Asked Questions About Stem Cell Therapy

What is stem cell therapy?

Stem cell therapy, also known as regenerative medicine, promotes the repair response of diseased, dysfunctional, or injured tissue using stem cells or their derivatives. These cells can generate healthy cells to replace cells affected by disease or injury.

What conditions can stem cell therapy help treat?

Stem cell therapy is being studied and used to treat joint pain, osteoarthritis, soft tissue injuries, and various musculoskeletal conditions. Research institutions like Johns Hopkins and Mayo Clinic are also studying applications for heart disease, diabetes, and neurological conditions.

How does stem cell therapy work?

Stem cells have the unique ability to develop into many different cell types. When introduced to damaged tissue, they can help regenerate and repair the area by developing into the specific cells needed, promoting natural healing and tissue regeneration.

Is stem cell therapy safe?

When performed by qualified medical professionals using proper protocols, stem cell therapy has shown a strong safety profile. Mayo Clinic research has found stem cell therapy to be safe, with studies showing benefits for conditions like spinal cord injuries and joint problems.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on the condition being treated and individual factors. Some patients notice improvements within weeks, while others may take several months to experience the full benefits as tissue regeneration occurs gradually.

Is stem cell therapy covered by insurance?

Most stem cell therapies for musculoskeletal conditions are not currently covered by insurance as they are considered investigational. We offer financing options and can discuss costs during your consultation.

What is the recovery time?

Stem cell therapy is minimally invasive, and most patients can return to normal activities within a few days. Your provider will give you specific post-treatment instructions based on the area treated.
