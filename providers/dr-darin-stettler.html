About Dr. Stettler

Dr. Darin Stettler is a dedicated osteopathic physician and surgeon committed to helping patients achieve optimal health and wellness. His approach to medicine emphasizes understanding the interconnected nature of the body's systems, allowing him to develop personalized treatment plans that address root causes rather than simply masking symptoms.

At Mint Medical Clinic, Dr. Stettler specializes in intimacy health, hormone optimization, and regenerative therapies. He understands that conditions affecting intimacy and vitality can be difficult to discuss, which is why he creates a comfortable, judgment-free environment where patients feel at ease sharing their concerns.

Dr. Stettler believes in empowering patients with knowledge about their health conditions and treatment options. He takes the time to thoroughly explain diagnoses, answer questions, and collaborate with patients to create treatment plans that align with their health goals and lifestyle.

Philosophy of Care

As an osteopathic physician, Dr. Stettler was trained to view the body as an integrated whole. This holistic perspective guides his approach to patient care, whether he's treating erectile dysfunction, optimizing hormone levels, or helping patients achieve their weight loss goals.

Dr. Stettler's treatment philosophy centers on three core principles:

  • Whole-person treatment — addressing root causes, not just symptoms
  • Patient empowerment — education and collaborative decision-making
  • Evidence-based care — combining proven treatments with innovative therapies
