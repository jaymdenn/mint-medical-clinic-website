Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the consultation take?

Plan for about 30-45 minutes for your initial consultation. This gives us enough time to thoroughly evaluate your needs and discuss all your options.

Is my visit confidential?

Absolutely. We take your privacy very seriously. All consultations and treatments are completely confidential, and we never share your information.

Do you accept insurance?

We operate on a self-pay model, which allows us to offer more personalized care and competitive pricing. We do offer flexible payment options.

Can I bring my partner?

Yes! We encourage it. Many of our treatments benefit both partners, and we're happy to address questions from both of you.

What if I prefer telehealth?

We offer full telehealth consultations for many services. You can meet with our physician via secure video call and have prescriptions shipped to your door.

How soon can I start treatment?

Many treatments can begin the same day as your consultation. For telehealth patients, medications typically ship within 1-2 business days.
